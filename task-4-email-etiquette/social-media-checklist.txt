Do's

-Utilize a professional profile picture on social media sites like LinkedIn to make a good first impression.

-Verify information before sharing news or academic sources to build credibility. 

-Utilize privacy settings to manage access to your personal posts and photos. 

-Network by establishing relationships with professionals in the field of Data Science. 

-Give credit to the creator of the content if you are sharing articles, code, or design inspiration in your social media feed.

Don'ts

-Sharing confidential information or internal college documents that are not meant to be publicly viewed

-Cyber bullying or the use of aggressive language within the comment section of a post

-Posting unprofessional content that can be viewed negatively by future internship coordinators

-Sharing your passwords or multi-factor authentication codes with anyone

-Clicking on suspicious links or "too good to be true" offers sent via direct messages
